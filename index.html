Who is estia?

Estia Building Maintenance Services Inc. is an incorporated building service contractor focused on providing top-knotch, closely supervised, janitorial services to clients in the Greater Toronto Area. Estia’s clients benefit from the experience of its cofounders: Jim Gallos, our President, established and successfully grew Apollo 8 into a successful company that serviced such industry giants as Oxford Properties Limited, Canderel, Bentall Kennedy and more; Cathy Pejovic’s expertise is earned through hands-on provision and supervision of operational efforts for a multitude of clients.

WHY estia?

Estia is a new entrant into the building service contractor market in the GTA. A highly crowded, competitive market, Estia’s founders recognized the need for an alternative for clients seeking a service provider with a smaller client base that could provide more high-level, high-standard care to select clients. Estia is uniquely positioned to provide such a service and be a partner of choice to Property Managers.

Estia offers clients a unique management style with a focus on customer satisfaction. Our most Senior Managers, our cofounders, are directly involved with each account, personally overseeing work processes and responding to customer requests and requirements. Our customers have our pledge: one-call, one-email and it will get done. Our customers can confidently feel that they are Estia’s number one priority – because they are.
